4 Leveraging Regulation as a Strategic Tool

Our investigation does not stop at understanding and complying with the regulations. Instead, we propose that companies can leverage regulation as a strategic tool, using it to guide their actions towards sustainable, ethical, and competitive practices within an AI-powered marketplace.

This book represents our collective effort to illuminate the intersection of AI, regulation, and competition, and we hope it will serve as a useful guide for all stakeholders navigating this rapidly evolving landscape.
